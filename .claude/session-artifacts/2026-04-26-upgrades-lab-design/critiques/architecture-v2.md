## New concerns introduced by v2

1. **Bash-as-curator-bootstrap** introduces a smaller coupling (script reads schema; schema doesn't read script). Right direction. But discipline-tag batch-retag on first MODULES.md ratification is a content rewrite the candidate underclaims as additive.
